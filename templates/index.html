🤖 Welcome to MoodSentry

MoodSentry is an intelligent emotion analytics system designed to monitor and improve emotional well-being in the workplace. Our platform uses cutting-edge technologies like facial recognition, voice tone analysis, and natural language processing to detect moods and assign appropriate AI-driven tasks to employees.

🚀 What You Can Do Here

  • Employees: Track your mood using text, voice, or facial inputs. Receive tasks tailored to your emotion.
  • HRs: Monitor your team’s emotional health, get stress alerts, and manage employee mood insights.
  • Owner: View detailed audit logs, access mood trends, and manage the entire workforce structure.

🎯 Key Features

  • 📊 Weekly mood analytics for teams and individuals
  • 📸 AI-based facial expression mood detection
  • 🧠 Real-time emotion-aware task assignments
  • 📢 Stress alert notifications to HRs
  • 📝 Mood filtering, task history, and audit logs
